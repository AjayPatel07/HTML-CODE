IPL Winners List 2023, Winner and Runner List from 2008 to 2023


The first season of the Indian Premier League, the world’s most entertaining T20 cricket league , was played in 2008 , a year before the Indian cricket team won the first edition of the World T20 in a thrilling final. When this league was started, hardly anyone would have guessed that later this league would become famous all over the world. The IPL started as an ambitious project 16 years ago and is now known as the best Twenty20 cricket league in the world. The IPL tournament has been organised 16 times since 2008. Here, we have given the list of IPL winners in the table which includes the winner, runner-up, venue, number of participating teams, Player of the Match and Player of the Series.
